ABOUT

Shweta Srivastava is a Mumbai based singer-songwriter. She has been a disciple of music for the majority of her existence. She enjoys performing songs while playing the piano or guitar and sings to express genuine emotions for the audience to connect with. This is what drives her and even holds a strong position in her songwriting.

Her debut single,'July 19’, was a contemporary pop/bluesy ballad with elements from Blues and Jazz. Her second release ‘Home’ was a groovy pop number reminiscing the comfort of all that feels like ‘Home’. Both the songs made their way into many reputed editorial playlists such as Spotify’s 'Fresh Finds India', Rolling Stone’s ‘Hit List’ and Apple Music’s playlists 'Future Hits, New Music Daily and Breaking Indian Independent’. She is a featured artist with India's only International Radio Channel 94.3 Radio One too.

Her latest single ‘SATURN’ unleashes everything powerful - emotions, vocals and production. The song creates a lush and ambient vibe that captures all the emotional highs and lows. The song has a strong melody backed with moody yet fresh vocal harmonies to complement the lyrical turmoil of illusory unrequited love.
